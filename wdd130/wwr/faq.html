Freguently Asked Quesions:

How long are the trips?

Depending on how fast the river is flowing; trips can take from 1 to 2 hours


Is there transport back to the parkinglot?
There is a bus that will take everyone back to the parking lot after the trip is done or family or friends can drive down the pickup spot.


What equipment is given and what will we need to do during the trip?


paddles and life jackets will be given though larger sizes are limited. 2 people will be required to be constantly rowwing during the trip.


How large can groups be? How many to a raft?

6 people can fit into a raft and there are 3 rafts. One quide is required for each raft so the largest groups we can handle are groups of 15.
