Content

Home page

The content for this page will include the hero, company mission statement, and information about the creek. The image will be image 1 and the mission statement will say: "Our mission is to provide families, freinds and induviduals with memories they will remember for the rest of their lives. Memories full of fun and excitment while also maintaining safety and an affordable price!" The rest of the page will be information on the creek as well as a link to an outside website:(https://www.americanrivers.org/river/east-rosebud-creek/). It Will also use image 2 to show off the creek.

Images for the Home page

Rivers

The content for the FAQ should probbaly not include images becasue the focus of this page will be information and not sensational. Though the logo may appear in the top of the page as well as the company name. Some frequently asked questions could be: How long are the trips? Whats days are the trips available? Is there transport from the end back to the start of the trip? What equipment is provided? How many people are in one raft? How large can the groups be?

Images for the FAQ

Rates&Dates

This page will have the dates of operation for the week and tells the user that holidays are unavailable. The rates for the weekend will be higher than they are for weekdays and children and teens will be less expensive than adults.
