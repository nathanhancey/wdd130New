Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to introduce someone who has never been to Rexburg to the area, history and things to do in town. The front page should give intro to the location, general history and a little bit about the local government. There will be two pages one about significant locations nearby like the campus and the st. Anthony Dunes. And another page will give the locations of local buisnesses, resturants, stores and hotels with a little description of each. At the end of looking through this site the user should have a good overveiw of the towns, history and things to do if they visit. The site should also make Rexburg look appealing to someone who might be looking around Idaho for places to visit.

Audience

The audience are people who are looking into Rexburg as a town in order to move here, visit or are just looking for things to do around town.

Content

Home page

History of Rexburg: Founded in 1883 by Thomas Edwin Ricks, Rexburg, Idaho, has a vibrant history. Rexburg was founded by settelers who set up on the banks of the Teton River. These setteler were LDS Church member who, in 1883 were instructed by Thomas E Ricks to settle the valley. Rexburg is also honored by the USS Rexburg, a Navy research ship named after the city. Just 5 years after setteling the locals founded Ricks Academy now BYU-Idaho. BYU-idaho has over 19,000 students atteding each semester. Explore Rexburg: Nestled at an elevation of 4,865 feet, Rexburg offers a unique geographical experience. The city sits atop a shield volcano, providing a picturesque backdrop. With its close proximity to the St. Anthony Sand Dunes, Yellowstone National Park's West Entrance, and the stunning Teton Range, Rexburg is an ideal base for exploring diverse natural wonders and recreational opportunities. The area's volcanic history has left a lasting impact, enriching the soil and making Rexburg renowned for its great Idaho potatoes.

Images for the Home page

Places of Interest

Explore St. Anthony Sand Dunes: Located near Rexburg, the St. Anthony Sand Dunes offer a thrilling desert experience in the heart of Idaho. Spanning over 10,600 acres, these expansive dunes provide a unique playground for outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy activities such as sandboarding, off-roading, and hiking in this vast sandy landscape. The dunes' ever-changing contours create a dynamic environment, making it an ideal destination for those seeking adventure and a break from the ordinary. St. Anthony Sand Dunes present a captivating blend of natural beauty and outdoor activities, making it a must-visit destination for an exhilarating escape. Discover Yellowstone National Park: Just a short drive from Rexburg, Yellowstone National Park is a world-renowned natural wonder. Home to iconic geysers, including the famous Old Faithful, and diverse wildlife such as bison, elk, and bears, Yellowstone offers a amazing blend of geothermal wonders and pristine landscapes. Visitors can explore geothermal features, picturesque waterfalls, and vast meadows. The park's extensive network of trails caters to hikers of all levels, providing an immersive experience in one of the most iconic national parks in the world. Yellowstone's unparalleled beauty and ecological diversity make it a must-see destination for nature lovers and our course the family.
