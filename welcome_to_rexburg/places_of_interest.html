Places of Interest

Explore St. Anthony Sand Dunes:

Located near Rexburg, the St. Anthony Sand Dunes offer a thrilling desert experience in the heart of Idaho. Spanning over 10,600 acres, these expansive dunes provide a unique playground for outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy activities such as sandboarding, off-roading, and hiking in this vast sandy landscape. The dunes' ever-changing contours create a dynamic environment, making it an ideal destination for those seeking adventure and a break from the ordinary. St. Anthony Sand Dunes present a captivating blend of natural beauty and outdoor activities, making it a must-visit destination for an exhilarating escape.

Discover Yellowstone National Park:

Just a short drive from Rexburg, Yellowstone National Park is a world-renowned natural wonder. Home to iconic geysers, including the famous Old Faithful, and diverse wildlife such as bison, elk, and bears, Yellowstone offers a amazing blend of geothermal wonders and pristine landscapes. Visitors can explore geothermal features, picturesque waterfalls, and vast meadows. The park's extensive network of trails caters to hikers of all levels, providing an immersive experience in one of the most iconic national parks in the world. Yellowstone's unparalleled beauty and ecological diversity make it a must-see destination for nature lovers and our course the family.
